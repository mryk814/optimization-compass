## Rights review

- Records are bibliographic and evidentiary metadata: title, author or
  organization, date, URL, a short supported-claim description, and quality or
  freshness status.
- The longest title is 59 characters and the longest supported-claim summary is
  53 characters in the audited CSV. The catalog does not contain article,
  paper, book, documentation, or source-code bodies.
- Source records point to third-party material; they do not import the rights
  to that material into the Optimization Compass licenses.
- Product and organization names identify sources and may be trademarks. No
  official logo is included in the audited source table.
- Original papers, textbooks, vendor manuals, official documentation, and
  repositories retain their own copyright and license terms.

## Exceptions and future additions

Third-party quotations, figures, screenshots, logos, or substantial excerpts
must not be added as ordinary CC BY 4.0 project content. A contribution that
needs one must record, next to the material:

1. rights holder and source URL or citation;
2. the third-party license or the legal exception relied upon;
3. required attribution and modification notice; and
4. the exact file or field to which the exception applies.

The release checklist requires this audit to be refreshed when source types or
redistributed third-party material change.
